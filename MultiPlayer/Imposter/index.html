    Imposter


    Impostor is a fun game where you’ll be travelling in space with the whole crew. There’s one issue however, which is the fact that there’s an impostor among you. Be sure to watch your surroundings, since if you find yourself alone with someone else in a room, it might be the impostor. Their goal is to kill the whole crew, including you. They won’t stop and won’t have any trouble with hurting you and damaging the ship. It will be difficult to find them, but you need to keep looking and watch out for any suspicious behavior. Will you manage to save the ship and the crew? Come on, let's see! The game is inspired by Among Us.


    Game Controls:


    Move: "ARROW KEYS"
