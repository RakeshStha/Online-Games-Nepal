    TEST YOUR BRAIN WITH THESE GAMES


    We collected some brain and board games that you can play as two player in a single game. We are presenting you eight brain / table game which are played by everyone fondly. You can play Chess, Tic Tac Toe, Checkers, Ludo, Connect 4, Snake and Ladders, Mancala and Math games in game box for free! If you cannot decide on which game you want to play, you can give a chance to spin the “Spin” to let it pick a game for you.


    Features:
    Chess: It is played 1 or 2 player, difficulty levels, 2D/3D game view options
    Tic Tac Toe: Played single or two player and you can create 3x3, 5x5, 10x10 boards.
    Checkers: 2 or 4 player game mode and crossed style game feature.
    Connect 4: It is played either single or two player and you can define line and column.
    Snake and Ladders: It is played by 1 player or 2 player or 3 player or 4 player.
    Mancala: It can be played by 1 player or 2 player, difficulty levels and different game themes.
    Math: 1 or 2 player feature, difficulty levels and question types (Addition, Subtraction, Division, Multiplication)
