    Baby Shark


    BabyShark is yet another fun online game where you will be controlling sharks. You start the game as a little shark and your goal will be to grow as large as possible. For you to be able to do so, you will need to eat as much food as possible by finding it or killing your enemies. You can only kill those who are smaller than you, so it won’t be as simple as it sounds. As you get rid of more and more enemies, you will accumulate more and more points for your scoreboard. In the menu, you will be able to customize your shark. So, good luck.


    Game Controls:


    - "MOVEMENT"


    Left click on mouse : "DASH"


    Have fun!
