    Eure Penalty 2021


    Prepare for the largest football event of 2021 - Euro Penalty Cup 2021! Become a player in the best European teams and lead your team to victory among the competition. Playing the game is rather easy. Try to score a goal by outsmarting the goalie with wild trick-shots. Don’t celebrate too early though, since you will also have to protect your own goalie. Try to outsmart your opponents and figure out their next move. Also, be cautious and take note of the red indicator that can help you by providing you with some tips. So take your football boots and show everyone who's the best by winning the finale!


    Game Controls:


    - Click on the mouse and follow the indicators.
