    Vector Rush


    Vector Rush is a parkour online game in which you control your character. It will constantly run, so your main goal will be to jump at the right time so that you can overcome various obstacles, jump between buildings and so on. A total of 15 levels are waiting for you, the difficulty of which will of course increase. For the coins earned, you can then unlock various tricks for your character and look like a bigger professional when jumping in the city. You can also play on your mobile, so let's get to it!
