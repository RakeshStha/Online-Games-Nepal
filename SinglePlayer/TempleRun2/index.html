    Temple Run 2


    The hero who was seeking treasure in a huge temple is in trouble in the Temple Run 2 game! The hero has a monster after him which is the protector of the treasure. There are many obstacles that await him on his way of run. He should both run, avoid obstacles, and collect power-ups on his way. If he can do third of these, it will be much easier to get rid of the character. You can upgrade the character's features by the coins you collect on his way while running from the "Abilities" menu in main menu.


    Game Controls:


    Move: "ARROW KEYS"
